This is the process I follow to make websites:


Asset Collection

The first phase is a review of all your current information, photos, case studies, testimonials... anything pertinent to your site goal is gathered and reviewed. This is the time to test all your information for pertinence and 'up-to-date-ness.'

Information Architecture

Now that we have all that information, it's time to review how it will best be organized for consumption by your target audience. Someone may come to your site looking for a calendar, a menu, directions to somewhere... Working to get them the information most efficiently makes your website great in their perspective.

Design Look and Feel

No kittens, no dancing rainbow images, no spinning email graphics. We can work towards a clean design that effectively transmits your brand or message. Color is good, readability and accessibility is better.

Build and Host

The last step is to bring the design to life and place your words on a real website! The details of where and how it's hosted are worked out here. The project will be reassesed to ensure it meets the project goals and requirements, then you're free to go!*

*That is, after you pay me of course!
